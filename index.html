Amity ── The Minecraft Utility Client
A new and upcoming utility client that is not only powerful, but also stylish!
Features
Amity client is a brand new, opensource project with a wide range of different features to suit all of your needs! It can offer you a variety of Combat, Movement, Player, and Rendering modules to enhance your gameplay! And not only is the Client's UI sleek and modern, it allows for the player to customise its colour scheme!
